Scoring system

Points are cumulative — a team earns points for its group finish, plus points for every knockout round it reaches. For example, a team that finishes 2nd in its group and goes on to win the tournament earns 3 (group) + 2 (R32) + 2 (R16) + 3 (QF) + 3 (SF) + 3 (F) + 2 (Winner) = 18 points.

Note on the Round of 32 bracket: FIFA's official rules use a 495-scenario lookup table to assign the 8 best third-placed teams to bracket slots, depending on exactly which groups they come from. This tracker uses a simplified assignment (best-ranked third-place team takes the first available "3rd place" slot, and so on) rather than the full official table, so a few R32 matchups may differ slightly from the real bracket.
